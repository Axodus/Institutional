# Publication Review Checklist

Use this checklist for human review. A checked box records inspection, not
automatic approval.

## Evidence and claims

- [ ] Every material Axodus statement has a claims-register classification.
- [ ] Blocked internal claims are absent from the manuscript.
- [ ] Facts, hypotheses, proposals, and future work are distinguishable.
- [ ] Prototype-stage status is explicit and consistent.
- [ ] No operational, adoption, security, financial, or novelty overclaim remains.

## References

- [ ] Each citation resolves to one verified BibTeX entry.
- [ ] Each BibTeX entry is used or intentionally retained.
- [ ] Metadata was checked against a primary record.
- [ ] No `[REFERENCE NEEDED]` marker remains.
- [ ] Sources support the propositions for which they are cited.

## Terminology and presentation

- [ ] Proof of Knowledge is distinguished from cryptographic proofs of knowledge.
- [ ] Human oversight and accountability boundaries are explicit.
- [ ] Markdown and LaTeX are structurally and substantively equivalent.
- [ ] Promotional and fundraising language is absent.

## Disclosure and rights

- [ ] No secrets, credentials, private repositories, infrastructure, or sensitive IP appear.
- [ ] Author, affiliation, correspondence, and license metadata are approved.
- [ ] Third-party content is properly attributed and legally usable.

## Release decision

- [ ] Editorial blockers are resolved in `publication-readiness.md`.
- [ ] A named human reviewer approved the exact release version.
- [ ] The intended venue and its policies were checked separately.
