The next patch must follow these mandatory rules:

- Any conceptual change in `paper.md` must be mirrored in `paper.tex`.
- Section headings must remain semantically equivalent across both files.
- The abstract, introduction, problem statement, conceptual architecture,
  evaluation plan, limitations, research agenda, and conclusion must be
  checked pairwise.
- No Markdown-only conceptual drift.
- No LaTeX-only conceptual drift.
- No bibliography or citation-key change unless separately approved.
- If a paragraph is changed in one manuscript format, the corresponding
  paragraph must be updated in the other.
- If exact paragraph matching is impossible, create a synchronization note
  documenting semantic equivalence.
