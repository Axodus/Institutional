# v0.5 Governance Scope Plan

Status: Phase 1.20 planning record  
Date: 2026-07-05  
Manuscript drafting authorized: no

## v0.5 paper-safe governance content

A future v0.5 paper may include only the following governance abstractions:

- constitutional center;
- bounded local governance domains;
- stakeholder proposal and refinement layer;
- community ratification and contestation layer;
- rapid risk-response and procedural review function;
- role-abstract primary AI assistance;
- role-abstract meta-supervision and adversarial review;
- participation-readiness support without token economics; and
- synthetic evaluation of review, escalation, and reconstructability.

## Content that must stay out of the paper

- token design, token distribution, supply, halving, liquidity, pricing, or
  valuation;
- named agents;
- product module catalog;
- exact governance thresholds or unresolved numerical parameters presented as
  fixed;
- revenue-split mechanics, percentages, or treasury logic;
- operational governance claims;
- deployment, customer, partner, or adoption claims; and
- legal or regulatory conclusions.

## Mapping from governance content to paper language

- `Executive` -> `rapid risk-response and procedural review function`
- `Boardroom` -> `stakeholder proposal and refinement layer`
- `Community` -> `community ratification and contestation layer`
- `Tenant` -> `bounded local governance domain`
- named agents -> `primary AI assistance` and `meta-supervision roles`

## v0.5 drafting preconditions

Before any v0.5 manuscript drafting begins:

1. The governance-mechanics public stub must exist and remain boundary-clean.
2. The tokenomics workstream must remain blocked from the public repo.
3. The paper-facing governance abstractions must be locked in terminology and
   claims records.
4. The v0.4.1 paper-line decision must be reviewed so v0.5 does not bypass an
   active manuscript correction path without explicit approval.
