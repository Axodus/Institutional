## Evaluation Criteria

Each candidate must be evaluated under the following conditions:

- the candidate remains non-citable until separately verified and promoted
  under `../../governance/citation-policy.md`;
- the candidate must have a bounded manuscript-facing purpose tied to an
  existing manuscript section;
- the candidate must not force new claims, new implementation implications,
  or Academy operational or financial drift;
- the candidate must not require manuscript restructuring just to justify
  inclusion; and
- the candidate may be useful to the research program without being approved
  for manuscript use.

Phase `2.2` does not make metadata-verification claims and does not make
citation-promotion claims.
