## Status Rules

- `PASS`: current internal evidence satisfies the planning criterion.
- `PENDING`: bounded work or evidence remains incomplete.
- `BLOCKED`: a required control or artifact does not yet exist or cannot
  proceed in this phase.
- `NOT_APPLICABLE`: criterion does not apply, with rationale required.
- `HUMAN_DECISION`: policy, legal, identity, disclosure, or authority choice
  cannot be made autonomously.

`PASS` does not authorize distribution. This matrix cannot issue
`PASS_AUTHORIZED`.
