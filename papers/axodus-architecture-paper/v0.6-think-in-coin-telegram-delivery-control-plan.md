# v0.6 Think in Coin Telegram Delivery-Control Plan

## Status

- Phase: 2.16
- Delivery authorized: no
- Reviewer outreach authorized: no
- Purpose: define controls for a possible separately authorized Phase 2.17 delivery

## Channel controls

A future delivery must use a private, access-controlled group or channel with named or otherwise approved and verified reviewers. Membership in Think in Coin is not by itself evidence of reviewer qualification. The setup must use no public invite link, limit administrative privileges, disable forwarding where the platform permits, pin the package instructions, define a review window, and prohibit reposting.

Telegram is only a communication channel. The repository or another controlled record remains the source of truth for package hashes, assignments, declarations, returned reviews, author dispositions, and closure decisions.

## Required pre-delivery checks

1. Phase 2.17 identifies each reviewer, eligibility basis, assigned language, package hashes, purpose, scope, review period, and return channel.
2. Conflict and confidentiality declarations are accepted and recorded.
3. Access is individual and least privilege; no public or anonymous shared link is used.
4. The pinned message identifies the English manuscript as authoritative and the pt-BR copy as a controlled translation.
5. Reviewers receive only the approved package, not repository-wide access, private branches, issues, credentials, infrastructure, or internal deliberations.

## Return and closure

Reviews must be returned through the assigned private channel or controlled form. Original returns are retained immutably and indexed without erasing disagreement. After the review window, access is removed or archived according to the Phase 2.17 record, and reviewers receive a retention or deletion request where applicable.

## Incident procedure

Unauthorized disclosure, forwarding, account compromise, or package-hash mismatch requires: suspend further delivery; preserve evidence; identify affected artifacts and recipients; notify the responsible human; revoke access where possible; record the incident; and issue a supersession or containment decision. No absolute confidentiality guarantee is claimed for Telegram.
