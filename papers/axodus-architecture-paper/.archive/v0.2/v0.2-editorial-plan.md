# v0.2 Editorial Plan

Planning date: 2026-07-03  
Scope: editorial direction only, not public-release preparation

## Objective

Produce a v0.2 manuscript direction that is narrower, more academically
defensible, and clearly framed as a prototype-stage conceptual architecture
and research-agenda paper suitable for author review.

## Editorial target

- Primary framing: internal working paper
- Secondary framing: conceptual architecture plus research agenda
- Non-targets for v0.2: public publication, submission, outreach,
  fundraising, grant use, or institutional authority signaling

## Core editorial decisions to lock before drafting

1. Approve one narrowed title.
2. Approve whether `Proof of Knowledge` is removed from public-facing
   manuscript language or retained only as a qualified internal label.
3. Approve replacement of `Academy` with `educational layer`.
4. Approve the out-of-scope boundary excluding tokenomics and token-release
   mechanisms from the architecture paper.

## Proposed v0.2 manuscript direction

- Keep Axodus framed as a prototype-stage conceptual model only.
- Recenter the paper on one explicit integrative gap:
  how governance, evidence, bounded AI assistance, educational participation,
  and risk boundaries can be described in one inspectable institutional model.
- Reduce branded or internally loaded terminology where a generic academic
  phrase is more defensible.
- Present participation-readiness as a contested educational/governance design
  proposal, not as a validated mechanism.

## Section-level plan

### Front matter

- Replace the current title with a narrower prototype-stage title.
- Keep the draft and not-ready-for-publication status explicit.

### Abstract

- Rewrite around gap, bounded contribution, and exclusions.
- Remove any wording that invites interpretation as an institutional launch
  document.

### Introduction

- Add a concise explicit research-gap paragraph.
- Compress the current multi-front opening into one integrative problem.
- End with a tighter contribution statement and explicit scope exclusions.

### Related work

- Keep socio-technical systems, DAO governance, human-AI oversight, and
  credentials/authorization distinction.
- Mark missing areas as literature gaps to close before any controlled
  external review.

### Conceptual architecture

- Retain the layered model.
- Clarify that layers are analytical and interface-level, not implemented
  subsystems.

### Participation section

- Prefer generic language such as `educational layer` and `participation
  readiness mechanism`.
- Keep the cryptographic disclaimer if the internal term appears at all.

### Risk section

- Narrow to institutional risk boundaries.
- Remove or minimize treasury-specific framing in the main narrative.

### Evaluation and future work

- Prioritize one near-term path, likely traceability and bounded human-AI
  review, before more ambitious participation-readiness work.
- Keep the rest as sequenced future work rather than co-equal commitments.

## Explicit out-of-scope boundary for v0.2

The architecture paper v0.2 must not describe the educational mechanism as:

- a token minting engine;
- a supply-control mechanism;
- a value-accrual or scarcity mechanism;
- an investment mechanism;
- a financial incentive model.

That entire design surface belongs, if ever documented, in a separate
PoK/tokenomics technical note after legal, economic, and empirical review.

## Supporting research tasks before stronger external positioning

- Verify literature on deliberative competence and informed participation.
- Verify literature on educational assessment validity, fairness, and
  accessibility.
- Verify literature on contestability and institutional appeals.
- Verify literature on empirical DAO participation and delegation.
- Verify literature on Web3 regulatory and operational constraints.

## Acceptance criteria for starting the v0.2 rewrite

- One narrowed title is approved.
- The manuscript language policy for `Proof of Knowledge` is approved.
- The manuscript language policy for `Academy` is approved.
- The author approves the no-tokenomics boundary for this paper.
- The paper remains classified as internal working paper / author-review only.

## Go / no-go recommendation

Go for v0.2 manuscript edits **only after** the acceptance criteria above are
explicitly approved. Until then, keep Phase 1.2 at the author-review gate.
