# Author Decision Record - v0.2

Decision date: 2026-07-03  
Scope: approved editorial direction before any v0.2 manuscript edits

## Purpose

This record captures the approved author-direction decisions for the Axodus
architecture paper before any v0.2 manuscript rewrite begins. It does not
authorize publication, submission, outreach, grants, fundraising use, or
public authority signaling.

## Decision status

These decisions are approved for manuscript-direction purposes only.
Publication blockers remain in force.

## Recorded decisions

1. **Title direction**

   The v0.2 title must explicitly signal that the manuscript is a
   prototype-stage conceptual architecture paper. Title language should narrow
   scope rather than imply a validated institutional model.

2. **Proof of Knowledge terminology status**

   `Proof of Knowledge` remains internal Axodus terminology for now. It should
   not be used as the primary public academic term in paper-facing text.

3. **Paper-facing PoK replacement policy**

   In manuscript-facing text, replace or qualify `Proof of Knowledge` with
   descriptive terms such as `knowledge-gated participation`,
   `educational participation mechanism`, or `governance-readiness
   mechanism`. If the internal label appears at all, it must be introduced as
   an Axodus-internal term with a strong disclaimer.

4. **Academy terminology policy**

   `Academy` should be replaced in manuscript-facing text with
   `educational layer` or `learning and credentialing layer`, except where it
   must be mentioned explicitly as an internal Axodus label.

5. **Tokenomics and token-linked participation boundary**

   The relationship between `Proof of Knowledge`, `$Neurons`, token minting,
   token supply, scarcity, incentives, and tokenomics is explicitly out of
   scope for the architecture paper. The paper must not describe the
   educational mechanism as a token minting engine, supply-control mechanism,
   scarcity engine, incentive mechanism, or financial model.

6. **`Learn to Win` terminology status**

   `Learn to Win` is a candidate UX/gamification motto only. It is not
   approved for academic terminology, tokenomics terminology, or public brand
   use before trademark and legal review.

7. **Use restriction**

   The architecture paper must not be used for publication, outreach, grants,
   fundraising, or authority signaling until a later readiness gate explicitly
   approves that use.

## Authorized next step

Focused v0.2 manuscript edits may begin after this decision record because the
required author-direction choices for title, terminology, and scope boundary
have now been recorded. This authorization is limited to editorial rewriting
inside the repository. It does not authorize any form of external circulation.
