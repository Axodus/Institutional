## Review mandate

Assess the manuscript only as a scientific artifact:

- central thesis and contribution;
- bounded novelty positioning;
- conceptual derivation and six-layer coherence;
- layer boundaries and literature relationship;
- claim-to-evidence alignment;
- evaluation design and falsifiability;
- limitations, scope, terminology, and rejection risks.

Do not assess product viability, production readiness, implementation infrastructure, tokenomics, treasury, fundraising, investor readiness, private governance, Academy operations, or commercial partnerships.
