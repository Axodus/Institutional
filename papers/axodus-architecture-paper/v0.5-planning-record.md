## Purpose

This record opens planning for a possible v0.5 paper line without opening
manuscript drafting. The immediate goal is to separate governance content into
the correct documentary tracks before any future paper rewrite begins.

## Decision

The next paper-track step is planning plus track separation, not direct paper
writing.

Three tracks are now distinguished:

- paper-track abstraction;
- public Institutional governance mechanics; and
- future tokenomics workstream, currently blocked from the public repo.

## Implication for the manuscript

The v0.4 manuscript remains the current internal draft baseline. A future
v0.4.1 remains planned but not opened until governance-track separation is
accepted. A future v0.5 may be considered only after governance content has
been sorted into the correct track and the paper-safe abstractions are
explicitly defined.

## Paper-track principle

The paper may absorb only governance material that strengthens the conceptual
argument about traceability, accountability, contestability, and bounded
human-AI-assisted governance. It must not absorb tokenomics, economic policy,
agent branding, or operational governance detail.
