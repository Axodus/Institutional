## Classification Rules

- `candidate`: may be prepared and evaluated internally.
- `pending human approval`: disclosure or metadata decision required.
- `internal supporting artifact`: retained for internal gate evidence.
- `requires redaction`: cannot become a review artifact in current form.
- `excluded`: must not enter the package.
- `blocked`: required artifact does not yet exist or is not approved.

## Approval Boundary

Only a later explicit human decision may change an artifact from `candidate`,
`pending human approval`, or `excluded by default` to an approved package
artifact. Package inclusion also requires a fixed version, hash, manifest,
approved protocol, and ER-8 decision.
